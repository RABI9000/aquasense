What this platform does

Four moving parts, one decision per day.
  • Satellite-driven moisture Sentinel-1 VV/VH bands calibrated against ground truth — moisture estimates that don't blink at clouds.
  • Uncertainty-aware prediction A 100-tree Random Forest returns a forecast and a confidence band. Irrigation triggers on the risk-adjusted value.
  • Live weather coupling Open-Meteo's 7-day forecast feeds the simulator — rain on day 3 means a smaller irrigation event on day 2.
  • Batch optimisation Compresses 196 micro-decisions into ~71 practical events without spilling into stress territory.
